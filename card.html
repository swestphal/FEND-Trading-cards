Octopus

Octopuses are highly intelligent.

  • Scientific Name: Octopus vulgaris
  • Average Length: Up to 4.3 meters
  • Average Lifespan: 6 months - 5 years
  • Habitat: Ocean

The octopus (/ˈɒktəpəs/ or ~/pʊs/) is a soft-bodied, eight-armed mollusc of the order Octopoda. Around 300 species are recognised and the order is grouped within the class Cephalopoda with squids, cuttlefish and nautiloids.
